About the Data

The JCVI priority groups data is from here. The number of individuals is estimated using data from the Office of National Statistics.

The number of vaccinations data is from here and is updated daily.

The graph assumes that there is a 100% take up of the vaccine. The graph also assumes that the vaccine is offered to the whole of a priority group before proceeding to the next. In reality, a number of groups will be vaccinated simultaneously.
